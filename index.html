Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 04, 2025

Ferue Shop ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our shop management application ("Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. This policy applies to business owners, employees, and authorized users of Ferue Shop.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

When you create an account or use the Service, we may collect:

  • Business Information: Shop name, address, tax ID, and contact details.
  • User Account Data: Name, email, phone number, and role (admin, cashier, etc.).
  • Payment Details: If integrated, payment processor data (we do not store full credit card numbers).

1.2 Operational Data

  • Inventory & Sales: Product details, transactions, customer purchases (non-identifiable).
  • Employee Activity: User logs, actions performed (e.g., sales processed).
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, app crashes (via Firebase Analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Service: Manage inventory, process sales, and generate reports.
  • To authenticate users: Role-based access control (RBAC) via Firebase Authentication.
  • To improve the app: Analyze usage patterns and fix bugs.
  • To comply with laws: Tax/financial reporting where required.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your data. Information may be shared only:

  • With Service Providers: Firebase (authentication), hosting providers (AWS/Google Cloud).
  • For Legal Compliance: If required by law (e.g., tax authorities).
  • Within Your Business: Admins can view employee activity and sales data.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (Firebase security rules).
  • Role-based access to sensitive data (e.g., only admins see financial reports).
  • Regular security audits of third-party providers.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you may:

  • Request access to or deletion of your data.
  • Update/correct account information in-app.
  • Contact us to revoke employee access.

6. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes via email or in-app alerts.
